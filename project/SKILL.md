## Quick reference

- **Type** — Nunito everywhere (900 display, 800 h2, 700 h3, 500 body). JetBrains Mono for `§` refs.
- **Brand color** — deep blue `#1B3A6B` (ink, headings, body).
- **CTA** — sunshine yellow `#FBC02D` with dark blue text — the brand's signature.
- **Voice** — warm, encouraging, "the smartest patient TA you ever had." Translate jargon, never water it down.
- **Iconography** — Lucide, 1.75–2px stroke, via CDN.
